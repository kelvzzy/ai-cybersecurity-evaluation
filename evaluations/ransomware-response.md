## Improved Answer
Immediately isolate affected systems from the network to prevent spread. Activate the incident response plan, preserve forensic evidence, and identify the attack vector. Do not pay ransom immediately. Assess backups and begin recovery if safe. Notify relevant stakeholders, including legal and cybersecurity teams. Conduct a full investigation and implement stronger security controls to prevent recurrence.
